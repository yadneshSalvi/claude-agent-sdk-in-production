Q2 2026 market roundup

The specialty coffee segment grew 6.8% in the second quarter of 2026 compared with the first, the third consecutive quarter of acceleration. Independent multi-store chains outperformed single-location shops for the fifth quarter running.

Cold beverages carried the quarter: iced and cold-brew programs grew 14% quarter over quarter across the panel, while hot espresso drinks were flat. Chains reporting the strongest growth attributed it to airport and campus locations recovering summer foot traffic.

Wholesale arabica costs eased 3% over the quarter, and most panel chains held retail prices steady, which lifted margins roughly a point. Panelists expect low-single-digit growth for Q3 as the summer slowdown offsets tourism traffic.
